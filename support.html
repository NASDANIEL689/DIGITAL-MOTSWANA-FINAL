Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my documents?

Go to the Documents section, select the document you want to verify, and follow the verification process. Make sure your documents are clear and legible.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, JPG, PNG, and TIFF formats. Files should not exceed 10MB in size.

How long does verification take?

Document verification typically takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive an email notification once the process is complete.
